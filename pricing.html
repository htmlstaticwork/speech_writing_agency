Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the questions our clients ask before their first engagement.

Is there a minimum engagement period?

The Event Single package has no minimum — it covers one standalone speech. The Campaign Series retainer is structured on a month-to-month basis with a recommended minimum of three months for narrative continuity.

How do you handle confidentiality?

Every engagement is protected by a mutual NDA signed before any brief is exchanged. We never disclose client identities, speech content, or strategic direction — before, during, or after the project.

What information do you need to begin?

We begin with a confidential intake call covering the occasion, audience, core message, and your preferred tone. From there, your assigned writer handles research, structure, and all drafts.

Can the timeline be expedited?

Yes. Rush timelines are available on all packages for an additional fee. Crisis Edge clients receive same-day drafting as standard. Contact us directly to discuss your deadline.
