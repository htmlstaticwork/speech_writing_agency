Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries about our editorial process and engagement protocols.

How confidential is the speechwriting process? +

Absolute discretion is our foundation. Every engagement is protected by a strict NDA. We never disclose client identities, speech content, or strategic details — before, during, or after delivery.

What is the typical turnaround for a speech? +

Standard engagements follow a two-week timeline from consultation to final draft. For crisis scenarios, our on-call team can deliver a polished script within 24 hours.

Do you offer delivery coaching alongside the written draft? +

Yes. Our Campaign Series and Crisis Edge retainers include one-on-one rehearsal sessions covering pacing, vocal cadence, teleprompter technique, and stage presence.

Can I request a specific writer for my project? +

Retainer clients may request a preferred writer. For single engagements, we assign the senior writer whose expertise best matches the domain and tone of your address.
